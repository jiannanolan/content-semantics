Beetroot (Beta Vulgaris)

  1. Common varieties

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest of the root.

The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or conbined with any salad vegetable.
